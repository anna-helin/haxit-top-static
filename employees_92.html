Background:
Cursed energy is used in this world for fighting against spirits by creating special attacks inherited by each person.
LL UMBRA is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world. UMBRA is the pride of the Gojo Clan, the first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years. He works as a teacher at the Tokyo Jujutsu High and uses his influence to protect and train strong young allies. His favorite food are hamburgers with japanese and italian cuisines also. He is a joyful and funny person that loves his friends and family. He is quite cocky but has a cold judgement in taking hard decisions. His favorite colour is purple.
Strengths:
Limitless technique grants its users nigh-absolute control over space through cursed energy manipulation at an atomic level, resulting in multiple subsequent results and techniques within the overall ability:

- Infinity being the neutral form of the Limitless, the Infinity is commonly known as the ability to stop any direct attack towards Umbra in a paradox. LL UMBRA is covered by Infinity.

- With Infinity Umbra can also Blue(lapse) and Red(reverse) objects or cursed energy near him.

- Hollow Purple being the ultimate attack by combining Red + Blue with cursed energy disintegrating anything in sight.

- Unlimited Void traps the enemy in a limitless domain. The trapped enemies are infused with Infinity making them unable to move or see inside the domain. This ability can only be used one time a day.
Weaknesses:
LL UMBRA being born with Six-Eyes(two special eyes) is a both a strength and a weakness. Although he can detect people's cursed energy and abilities, is bad in detecting physical attacks towards him. In normal conditions Infinity can stop any physical attack towards him even in his sleep by being always on, but an attack that controls/cuts through space and time bypasses Infinity with ease. The attack being a physical one would make it even unseen for LL UMBRA.

While using his Unlimited Void, LL UMBRA is inside the domain with the enemy. From outside the domain, it looks like a big sphere that can easily be broken from rocket like attacks. After using his Unlimited Void, LL UMBRA can not use it again for at least 24 hours.

He is quite cocky thinking he is the strongest in any situation. Can get caught by surprise.
